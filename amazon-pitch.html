🤝 Local Partnerships

Businesses form cross-promotional partnerships. A restaurant partners with event venues. A salon partners with a spa. BizHub facilitates these connections, creating a business network effect. Database table and dashboard built.

📢 Micro-Influencer Tracking

Every Proof of Visit post generates a unique share link. When that link drives views, the poster earns "impact" points. This gamifies word-of-mouth and turns every customer into a micro-promoter. Referral tracking live.

⚡ Live Pulse (Flash Deals)

Time-limited deals with urgency counters. Businesses clear stock, drive foot traffic during slow hours. Animated "LIVE PULSE" badges create FOMO. Full deal engine with expiry tracking deployed.

📅 Community Events Hub

Central calendar for everything happening in town — from church fundraisers to club nights. Event cards with date, time, location, pricing, and attendee counts. Drives weekend traffic. Events platform live with filters.

Risk: Verification Cost Sinks Margins

Physical verification is the trust moat, but scaling boots on the ground to 50+ towns carries heavy operational overhead.

Mitigation:

Transition to a hybrid model. Physical verification for the first 100 anchor businesses per town, then tech-driven verification (KRA PIN, live video) for the rest.

Risk: Google & Facebook Strike Back

Mega-corporations could theoretically launch hyperlocal, verified tools aimed at African towns.

Mitigation:

Hyperlocal networks are hard for global giants to manage. By capturing merchants securely and locking them in via the Reverse Marketplace, we raise switching costs.
