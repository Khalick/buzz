# 🎤 The 5-Minute Amazon Pitch Script

Use this exact script for verbal presentations (Zoom calls, in-person meetings). It is strictly timed to 5 minutes so you maintain control, skip fluff, and get directly to the strategic value—exactly what Amazon executives respect.

---

## 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook (Slide 1)
"Hi, I’m Peter Agak. I’m the Solo Founder & CEO of BizHub. I'm building Amazon's gateway to 7.9 million offline SMEs in Africa. We digitize and verify local commerce so that Amazon can scale its logistics and AWS ecosystem seamlessly across 290 secondary Kenyan towns, without the decade of groundwork it usually takes to map offline Africa."

## 0:30 - 1:30 | The Problem & Real Timing (Slide 2 & 3)
"Africa is a $4.1 trillion consumer market by 2030, but right now, the gap is massive. 90% of local businesses are completely invisible. Scams are rampant, forcing consumers to abandon digital trust. This isn't just a merchant problem—it’s an infrastructure problem. Mobile penetration is at 93%. The connectivity is there, the intent is there, but the trusted local discovery layer—the actual pipes of commerce—is broken."

## 1:30 - 2:30 | The Solution & The Moat (Slide 4, 6)
"That's exactly what BizHub provides. We are a hyper-local, AWS-powered infrastructure layer operating in these secondary markets. 

How do we do it? We physically verify every merchant. It starts manual and transitions to strict digital KYC. We combine this with an AI-powered conversational search engine built on Amazon Bedrock. 
When we map the merchants, we don't just put them on a directory—we lock them in. We provide them with analytics, flash-deal tools, and direct customer leads. Verified businesses increase consumer trust, which drives more transactions, creating a highly sticky, predictable operational cycle."

## 2:30 - 3:15 | The Undeniable Proof (Slide 10)
"This isn’t a concept; it’s working right now. In our first pilot town, Thika, we launched with a single codebase. We onboarded over 150 verified businesses, acquired 2,400 monthly active users, and achieved a staggering 12% premium conversion rate among merchants, driving 1.2M KES in projected revenue. We proved that African SMEs will pay for a verified, trusted platform."

## 3:15 - 4:15 | The Amazon Hook (NEW Slide 14)
"So, why are we speaking today? Because Africa is the final frontier for e-commerce and logistics. Amazon has immense power, but penetrating offline local markets town-by-town is notoriously expensive and slow. 

You need pre-verified merchants. You need on-ground trust. BizHub has already built this. 
We act as the ultimate strategic partner. We can seamlessly plug into Amazon's expansion plans, acting as the local supply chain node, accelerating AWS adoption at the ground level, and instantly onboarding local SMEs into your global network. We reduce market entry costs by years."

## 4:15 - 5:00 | The Ask (Slide 13)
"I built and architected this entire platform myself, scaling it up to what it is today. But to capture the other 289 towns in Kenya, we need scale capital and structural backing. 

We are seeking a strategic partnership with Amazon—specifically aligning on AWS infrastructure and marketplace integration—alongside a $500,000 pre-seed investment. We have the blueprint, the tech, and the traction. I’m ready to show you how we can capture Africa’s local economy together."
