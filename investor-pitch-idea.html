🔄 Innovation

The Reverse Marketplace & Growth Features

Three proprietary innovations that no competitor has: Smart Leads (reverse marketplace), Local Partnerships, and Micro-Influencer tracking.

💡 Smart Leads — "Reverse Market"

Instead of customers searching for businesses, customers broadcast their needs and businesses compete to respond. This flips the marketplace and creates high-intent leads.

🤝 Local Partnerships

Businesses form cross-promotional partnerships. A restaurant partners with event venues. A salon partners with a spa. BizHub facilitates these connections, creating a business network effect.

📢 Micro-Influencer Tracking

Every Proof of Visit post generates a unique share link. When that link drives views, the poster earns "impact" points. Gamified word-of-mouth turns every customer into a micro-promoter.

⚡ Live Pulse (Flash Deals)

Time-limited deals with urgency counters. Businesses clear stock, drive foot traffic during slow hours. Animated "LIVE PULSE" badges create FOMO and drive instant action.

📅 Community Events Hub

Central calendar for everything happening in town — from church fundraisers to club nights. Event cards with date, time, location, pricing, and attendee counts.
